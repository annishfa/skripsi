# Occupational Stress Risk Mapping

This document provides an interpretative mapping of job roles and industries 
to potential stress risk factors based on dataset variables.

---

## A. Job Role → Stress Risk Mapping

| Job Role | Job Characteristics | Relevant Dataset Variables | Type of Stress Risk |
|-----------|--------------------|----------------------------|---------------------|
| Project Manager | Team coordination & high responsibility | Hours_Worked, Number_of_Virtual_Meetings, Work_Life_Balance | Responsibility overload |
| Sales | KPI & performance targets | Work_Life_Balance, Company_Support | Performance pressure |
| Software Engineer | Intensive coding & long screen time | Hours_Worked, Sleep_Quality, Social_Isolation | Cognitive overload |
| Data Scientist | Complex analysis & project deadlines | Hours_Worked, Sleep_Quality | Mental fatigue |
| Marketing | Campaign deadlines & performance metrics | Work_Life_Balance, Hours_Worked | Time pressure stress |
| HR | Employee interaction & conflict handling | Social_Isolation, Company_Support | Emotional exhaustion |
| Designer | Creative production & client revisions | Work_Life_Balance, Hours_Worked | Creative fatigue |

---

## B. Industry → Stress Pattern Mapping

| Industry | Sector Characteristics | Dominant Dataset Variables | Potential Stress Pattern |
|------------|----------------------|--------------------------|--------------------------|
| IT | Remote-heavy & digital intensive | Hours_Worked, Social_Isolation | Overwork & isolation stress |
| Consulting | Client pressure & tight deadlines | Hours_Worked, Work_Life_Balance | Workload stress |
| Finance | Target-driven & high precision | Work_Life_Balance | Performance stress |
| Healthcare | High emotional labor | Social_Isolation | Emotional exhaustion |
| Education | Cognitive workload | Hours_Worked | Mental fatigue |
| Retail | Customer service interaction | Company_Support | Interaction stress |
| Manufacturing | Operational & structured workflow | Hours_Worked | Operational stress |

---

### Important Note

This occupational mapping is used strictly for interpretative analysis 
and discussion purposes. It is not used as a training label 
in the machine learning classification model.
